OVERVIEW

History

JCC is a famous conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. JCC is one of the earliest as well as popular convention centers in Jakarta. First established in 1974, JCC has held more than 10.000 events over the last 49 years. JCC were originally designed to accommodate 62 head of state and their delegations for the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and Asian-African Conference. Since then, JCC have kept hosting for many important national and international conference, exhibition, fair, indoor sports, and musical concerts. It is a very venue place throughout the year.

Why JCC

JCC is located on the heart of Jakarta’s central thoroughfares, which has an easy access to the international airport, the Jakarta central business district, the presidential palace, central and municipal administrative buildings, as well as many city’s prime shopping centers, restaurants and hotels. JCC also have over 49 years of experience, including a client portfolio such as presidents, executives, and international musicians. And also engagements portfolio such as world conventions, regional conferences, business meetings, festival, concerts, fashion shows, sports event, weddings, and exhibitions.

Facilities and services

JCC has over 13 event rooms, 4 of which have a flexible dimensions and capacities. There is 5.000 seat planetary hall, a round shape theatre venue, a assembly hall which can accommodate 2.500 seat for sit-down and 4.500 for buffets, the 2500 garuda room with movable floor to ceiling partitions, and the last nine is a meeting room. JCC also have 2 exhibition halls, each with a 1.000 sqm lobbies and access points. Which equipped with telephone and communication outlets, audio visual points, single and 3-phase power sources, and floor box to access electricity, phone lines, and water. JCC also provide a catering services, which has a spacious 1.500 sqm modern kitchen and the capacity to cater over 10.000 guests.

Certified

JCC is proud to announce that we have passed an in-depth set of assessments to achieve the honor of ISO 22000 : 2018. This allowed us to constantly improve our quality of system management and the process of our organization. This certification shows that we truly committed ti the quality of our services, as part of our business culture and philosophy.
