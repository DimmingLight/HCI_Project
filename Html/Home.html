The best convention in Jakarta

JCC is a famous conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. JCC is one of the earliest as well as popular convention centers in Jakarta. First opened in 1974, JCC has held more than 10.000 events over the last 49 years and host for many important national and international conference, exhibition, fair, indoor sports, and musical concerts including 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and Asian-African Conference. It is a very venue place throughout the year. JCC have been delighted to work with presidents, CEOs, and international musicians, as well as companies from almost all kind of industries. JCC provide extensive exhibition, event, and meeting space including state of the art sound, lightning, catering, and communication facilities with ethernet access throughout. To help design and direct events, JCC dedicated Convention and Exhibition Services Managers guide customers through each step of their projects. From accessories such as flower arrangements to sophisticated lighting management and contingency planning, JCC Convention and Exhibition Services Managers ensure all details and requirements are met.
